How to Cook a Steak

A basic recipe on how to cook the best meat in the world.

What You Need

  • Steak, Filet Mignon
  • Salt & pepper
  • Basil and Oregano
  • Virgin Olive oil
  • Pan & tongs

Steps

  1. 1 Bring steak to room temperature (20–30 min)
  2. 2 Pat dry and season with salt and pepper
  3. 3 Sprinkle with basil and oregano; make sure to do both sides
  4. 4 Heat pan until hot and add oil and butter
  5. 5 Sear first side 1.5-2 minutes; make sure it is properly cooked with a thermometer
  6. 6 Flip and sear the other side for 1.5-2 minutes
  7. 7 Rest the steak 5–10 minutes before slicing
